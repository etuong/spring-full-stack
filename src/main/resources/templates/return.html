RETURN POLICY

How do I return items?

BookSmart accepts returns of unworn, unwashed, undamaged and/or defective merchandise for a full refund within 30 days of purchase. Refunds will be made in the form of the original payment. All returned items must be accompanied by a return form.

What are the delivery charges?

BookSmart charges $5 and $10 for all domestic and international shipping, respectively.
